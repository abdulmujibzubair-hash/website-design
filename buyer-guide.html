Buyer Safety Guide

Before You Buy/Contact a Seller

  1. Verify product details before making payment.
  2. Read the product description carefully.
  3. Check the product condition (New, UK Used, Fairly Used).
  4. Verify the asking price.
  5. Look at all available photos.
  6. Check whether the product is still available.

Meeting Sellers Safely

  1. Meet sellers in public places on campus.
  2. Meet during daytime when possible.
  3. Avoid isolated locations.
  4. Inform a friend if meeting a seller.

Payment Tips

  1. Inspect the item before sending money.
  2. Do not rush into transactions.
  3. Verify account details before transfer.
  4. Keep proof of payment.
  5. Save screenshots of conversations.
  6. Avoid transactions outside agreed communication channels.

Understanding CampusPlug

  1. CampusPlug is a platform that connects buyers and sellers.
  2. CampusPlug does not handle payments or inspect products.
  3. Helps students discover products and services.
  4. Promotes student entrepreneurship.
  5. Does not physically inspect products.

NOTE:

CampusPlug connects buyers and sellers but is not directly involved in transactions. Buyers are encouraged to verify products, inspect items, and exercise caution before making payments.
